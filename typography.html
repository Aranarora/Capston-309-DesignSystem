Typography

Intro:

Playfair Display is RunRider's special Typeface. It has a fancy look with fun, rounded edges that shows RunRider is a trustworthy, optimistic, and sincere company. The font is also light, giving it a clean and stylish look. All of these cool features make Playfair Display perfect for RunRider's headings and important text.

Supporting Typeface is:

Montserrat is the font we use a lot, it's simple and easy to read. It helps balance out the fancy look of Playfair Display and makes sure our text is clear and easy to understand. It's like a workhorse, always there to support and make things run smoothly.
