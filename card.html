Card

A "card" is a compact rectangular unit that represents a single idea. Cards typically contain interactive elements such as text, links, buttons, or images, but their main purpose is to provide a brief overview of a topic and allow for easy exploration by clicking through to more information on another page. The goal of cards is to present information succinctly and offer a quick way to delve deeper into the subject.
