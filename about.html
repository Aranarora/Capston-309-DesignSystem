RunRentals Journey

We want to alter the way people in Comox Valley travel and experience the area. Go Scoot is pleased to provide a dependable, emission-free e-scooter to make your journey simple and enjoyable.

We first became passionate about e-scooters since we were so bored and restless during the pandemic shutdown. We were captivated as soon as we climbed on these strong, two-wheeled marvels! How incredible! Courtenay is a stunning city that you could easily and swiftly tour.

From this wonderful encounter, RunRentals was created. In the spring of 2022, we opened our storefront in Comox Valley, the heart of downtown Courtenay.
